Flower Power!

Save the bees!

Bees are an important part of the pollination process. Their survival and ours depends on their ability to collect pollen from plants.

Help these bees meet their pollination goals!
In each game, the bees need to collect exactly enough pollen to reach their Pollen Goal. Each flower is assigned a random pollen score.
Click on a flower to collect it's pollen.
Try to meet the Pollen Goal exactly. If you go over the Pollen Goal, you lose! The pollen values for each flower will change with every game.
